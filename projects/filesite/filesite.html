FILE.SITE

UI/UX, WEB DESIGN, DEVELOPMENT

File.site is an online website that allows users to transfer files to different devices or to others. Read below to learn more about the why it was created, what went into its design, and how it was ultimately developed.

FILE SHARING CAN BE CONFUSING, AND EXPENSIVE.

File sharing can be difficult, whether its sending a photo to your whole family or a whole folder to a coworker. While emails and storage websites do work, they typically have a limit on how much you can transfer, or require an account/subscription.
