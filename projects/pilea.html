PILEA

GRAPHIC DESIGN, UI/UX, APP DESIGN

Pilea is a plant care app prototype created in Adobe XD. The app allows houseplant owners to add their plants and get reminded when and how they should be cared for. The design utilizes original iconography and illustrations to create a stress-free environment for a sometimes forgetful task.

ORIGINAL ICONOGRAPHY

Pilea features a series of original icons used throughout the app. These icons provide a cohesive feel no matter what action or menu the user is accessing. The icons were created with scalability in mind in order for them to be used anywhere, wether this is the app's logo or even a small menu button.
