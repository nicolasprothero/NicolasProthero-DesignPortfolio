SPROUT SHOOTER, 2022

JAVSCRIPT, HTML, CSS

Sprout Shooter is a web game, coded entirely in Javascript and HTML/CSS, that can be played on a web browser. All original hand drawn sprites and images. Features a functioning score, level, and sharing feature.

DESIGN WORK

PILEA, 2022

UI/UX, APP DESIGN, PROTOTYPING, ICONOGRAPHY

Pilea is a plant care app prototype created in Adobe XD. The app allows houseplant owners to add their plants and get reminded when and how they should be cared for. The design utilizes original iconography and illustrations to create a stress-free environment for a sometimes forgetful task.

TALKGT LOGO, 2022

LOGO DESIGN, GRAPHIC DESIGN

TalkGT.com is a communication website that allows Georgia Tech students to submit posts and requests online. The logo which blends both the school's mascot, Buzz and a message bubble was created to embody the purpose of the website.

RED BEAR FISHING CO, 2022

GRAPHIC DESIGN, ILLUSTRATION, BRAND DESIGN

Red Bear Fishing Co. is a fictional outdoor lifestyle brand. Red Bear specializes in selling outdoor backpacking equipment and clothing. The brand focuses on a playful and adventerous design that reflects their ideals.

TINNED ON TENTH, 2022

LOGO DESIGN, BRAND DESIGN
